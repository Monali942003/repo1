VISSION

To have a sustainable, efficient, safe and internationally comparable quality of road infrastructure in general and National Highways infrastructure in particular to achieve enhanced connectivity, quick mobility to a level which accelerate socio-economic development

MISSION

OUR ROAD SAFETY VISSION:
  • To be recognised worldwide for our work in saving lives through enabling smarter enforcement, changing driver behaviour for the better and ensuring those who break the law are dealt with effectively..
  • Develops roads for the road users respectful, inclusive, and driven through a supportive environment.
  • Utilizes the involvement of students, families, and staff to create a community that contributes to society.
